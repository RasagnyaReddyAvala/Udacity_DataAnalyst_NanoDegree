Female gender is dominant in both turning up and not showing up for their appointments.

In Maria Ortiz neighbourhood people who received SMS but didnt show up(641) are larger in number than people who actually didnt receive any SMS and hence didnt show up

Limitations¶

Although we made some interesting observations, the features here limit us to find the actual reason behind no show appointments.

Additional information like climatic conditions, distance between the patient residence and hospital, previous appointments and customer service might help in deriving interesting insights
